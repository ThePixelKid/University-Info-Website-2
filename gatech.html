Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech)

Location: Atlanta, Georgia, USA

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1885
  • Type: Public research university (top-tier engineering school)
  • Enrollment: ~43,000 (includes graduate students)
  • Known for: Engineering, Computer Science, Technology research

Programs

  • B.S. in Computer Science (threads: Devices, Info Internetworks, Intelligence, Systems & Architecture)
  • M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science (including Software Systems specialization)
  • Online offerings: OMSCS (Online Master of Science in Computer Science)

Tuition & Costs (approx.)

  • In-state: ~$10,500 / year
  • Out-of-state: ~$33,500 / year
  • Note: Costs vary by program and year â€” check official site for current rates.

Admissions

Highly selective for CS and engineering programs. Typical evaluation includes transcripts, test scores (if submitted), letters of recommendation for graduate applicants, and demonstrated research/work experience for advanced degrees.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: World-class STEM reputation, strong industry and alumni network, excellent placement and research opportunities.
  • Cons: Very competitive admissions, heavy workload, higher out-of-state tuition.
