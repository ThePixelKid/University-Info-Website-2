University of Houston

Location: Houston, Texas, USA

Quick Facts

  • Type: Public research university
  • Enrollment: ~46,000
  • Affordable and diverse; strong applied CS programs

Tuition & Costs

  • In-state: ~$8,000/year
  • Out-of-state: ~$22,000/year

Admissions

Accessible programs with emphasis on practical software engineering and testing.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Affordable, diverse campus, practical focus.
  • Cons: Less national elite recognition than top private schools.

Career Outcomes

  • Average starting salary for CS grads: ~$78,000.
  • Good regional placement within Houston's diverse industries.

Notable Programs & Strengths

  • Applied CS programs, industry partnerships, and practical training.
