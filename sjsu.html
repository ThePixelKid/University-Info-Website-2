San Jose State University (SJSU)

Location: San Jose, California, USA

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1857 (one of the oldest CSU campuses)
  • Type: Public university (California State University system)
  • Enrollment: ~33,000

Programs

  • B.S. in Software Engineering
  • M.S. in Software Engineering
  • Practical, career-oriented curricula with strong employer connections

Tuition & Costs (approx.)

  • In-state: ~$8,000 - $12,000 / year
  • Out-of-state: ~$25,000+ / year

Admissions

Accessible and practical choice for students targeting Bay Area industry experience; strong internship pipelines with major local employers.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Located in Silicon Valley; excellent internship and job pipeline; more affordable than private options.
  • Cons: Large commuter population; campus resources differ from larger research universities.

Career Outcomes

  • Average starting salary for grads in software fields: ~$95,000.
  • High internship conversion into full-time roles within Bay Area companies.

Notable Programs & Strengths

  • Strong practical software engineering programs and industry-facing coursework.
