Texas Tech University

Location: Lubbock, Texas, USA

Quick Facts

  • Type: Public research university
  • Enrollment: ~40,000
  • ABET-accredited CS and software engineering programs

Tuition & Costs

  • In-state: ~$9,000/year
  • Out-of-state: ~$23,000/year

Admissions

Project-based learning focus with practical software engineering courses.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: ABET-accredited programs, practical projects.
  • Cons: More regional focus; less Silicon Valley pipeline.

Career Outcomes

  • Average starting salary: ~$72,000 for software and engineering grads.
  • Good local/regional employer connections for practical placements.

Notable Programs & Strengths

  • ABET-accredited CS and software engineering with strong project-based learning.
